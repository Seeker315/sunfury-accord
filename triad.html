Governing Triad

The Triad

Three branches. One premise. No single hand above the others.

The Accord is governed by a Triad of three equal members, each directing one branch. Authority belongs to the individual, not the title. In the event of a deadlock, the founding member holds a tiebreaking function by personal designation.
