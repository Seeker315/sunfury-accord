Guild Structure

The Governing Triad
& Operational Framework

The Accord is not a hierarchy built on worth. It is a structure built on function. Each branch serves a purpose. No branch outranks another.

The Triad — Governing Council
Archon
Seeker Branch
Directs the Seekers. Embodies wisdom and the mastery of power. The investigative arm of the Accord.
Paragon
Vanguard Branch
Directs the Vanguard. Embodies willpower, resolve, and the commitment to see things through.
Consul
Cipher Branch
Directs the Ciphers. Embodies diplomacy, connection, and the unseen architecture of the Accord.

In the event of a deadlock, the founding member holds a tiebreaking function by personal designation. This authority belongs to the individual, not the title.

Initiation & Advancement
I
Application
The applicant introduces themselves and their character. A brief conversation with the Triad or a designated member follows. An invitation as Adherent is extended or not. No open mass recruitment.
II
The Adherent Phase
The Adherent prepares a Trial of their own choosing, as long as it connects thematically to the Accord's premise. A magical theory. A tactical thesis. A network concept. The standard is intention, not quality. After four weeks without contact, a conversation is initiated. After six weeks without result, the Adherent phase ends without drama.
III
Presentation & Full Admission
The member announces their presentation to the Triad. The guild assembles. The presentation is held and the Accord formally admits the member with the appropriate entry rank. This moment is deliberately public. It is the first social anchor of membership.
IV
Advancement to Tier II
Advancement does not happen through attendance or time served. Those who wish to advance approach the Triad themselves. A second presentation is required: a demonstration of depth and development. The Triad decides. No guaranteed timeline.
